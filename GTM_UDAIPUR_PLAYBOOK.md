# Zuno GTM – Udaipur Launch Playbook (Shareable)

## Launch thesis
Zuno should win by density, not breadth.
Start with **one live zone** and create repeatable loops.

## Phase rollout
1. **Phase 1:** Udaipur, Goa, Manali
2. **Phase 2:** Rajasthan + Himachal clusters
3. **Phase 3:** South travel cluster (Gokarna, Hampi, Varkala)
4. **Phase 4:** Metro scale-up

## Udaipur street-level zone (Day 1 focus)
- Old City triangle:
  - Lake Pichola
  - Jagdish Temple
  - Gangaur Ghat

## Seeding plan
- Seed **15–20 plans/day** manually in first 2 weeks.
- Plan archetypes:
  - Sunrise
  - Sunset
  - Cafe hops
  - Social dinners
  - Rides

## Distribution channels
- Hostel partnerships (Zostel-like, Moustache-like, GoStops-like)
- QR placement message:
  - “Find people to explore with today”
- Ask each hostel to post 1–2 plans daily.

## Product metrics to watch
- `% users joining a plan within 2 minutes`
- Target: **>= 30%**

## Product requirements implemented in app
- City-first top selector in feed
- “Happening next” slot by selected city
- Quick join / leave flow
- Saved plans flow
